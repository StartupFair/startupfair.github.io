For Students

The BU Startup Fair is a chance for students and startuppers to communicate one-on-one in a fun environment. We hope not only to match job-seekers with jobs, but also to create lasting relationships between BU students and Boston startups.

For free?

Yep!! Tables, pizza, soda, cookies...it's all on us. Startups save hundreds of dollars on attendance fees and students get a free dinner.
